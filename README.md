## What Are Kratom Shots

**Kratom shots** represent a concentrated liquid form of kratom extract, typically sold in small bottles ranging from 1-3 fluid ounces. These products contain alkaloids extracted from the leaves of Mitragyna speciosa, a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia. Unlike traditional kratom powder that requires mixing with liquids, shots offer a ready-to-consume format.

The manufacturing process involves extracting key alkaloids like mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine from kratom leaves using various solvents and concentration methods. This results in a liquid product that delivers higher alkaloid concentrations in smaller volumes.
